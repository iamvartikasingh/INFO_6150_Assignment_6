About Boston

Learn more about the history and culture of America's oldest city.

Boston's History

Boston is one of the oldest cities in the United States, with a rich history that dates back to the 17th century.

Culture & Heritage

The city boasts a diverse culture influenced by a range of ethnicities and historical events, making it a cultural hub.

Famous Landmarks

From the Freedom Trail to Fenway Park, Boston is home to some of the most iconic landmarks in the US.

Education

Boston is a global leader in higher education, with prestigious institutions like Harvard, MIT, and Boston University.
